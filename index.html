Our Philosophy

The College of Applied & Collaborative Studies (CACS) prepares students to engage with a rapidly changing environment through partnership and innovation. In collaboration with industry partners in the DFW metroplex, CACS creates ongoing opportunities for students to learn and grow in today's workforce through project-based learning.

PROJECT BASED LEARNING

Project Based Learning is the cornerstone of our curriculum. Students work on real-world projects throughout the semester with guidance from professors, gaining hands-on experience that prepares them for their careers.

FUTURE STUDENTS
